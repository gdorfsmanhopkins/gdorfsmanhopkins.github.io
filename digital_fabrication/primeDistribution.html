Distributions of Small Primes

To test out the resolution and settings of the laser cutter I represented each integer with a thickness under a tenth of a millimeter, and then had the laser cutter draw a vertical line for each prime integer. This gives a nice feeling of the randomness and yet the density of the small primes. It seems to start to go far enough to show that the density is decreasing, but slowly.
