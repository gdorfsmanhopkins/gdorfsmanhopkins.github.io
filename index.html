I'm an assistant professor of mathematics at St. Lawrence University. My research spans algebraic geometry, arithmetic geometry, mathematical illustration, and digital fabrication, combining theoretical mathematics with visualization, computation, artistic practice, and physical making.

Alongside my research, I organize international initiatives including the Illustrating Mathematics Seminar Online and the 2026 Institut Henri Poincaré trimester Illustration as a Mathematical Research Technique. I also co-founded the Explore Lab Makerspace, an interdisciplinary space supporting creative scholarship across the St. Lawrence campus.

This website collects my mathematical research, artistic work, software, interactive visualizations, and collaborations with students.
