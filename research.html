Research

I've been working most recently studying Picard groups of perfectoid spaces, and more generally studying vector bundles and cohomology on perfectoid covers of varieties and schemes, specifically how to parametrize such objects and how they behave across characteristics (i.e., under tilting). Here is a selection of papers and preprints:

  • Untilting Line Bundles (2020, in preparation).
  • On Picard Groups of Perfectoid Covers of Toric Varieties (2020, preprint, joint with Anwesh Ray and Peter Wear).
  • Projective Geometry for Perfectoid Spaces (2019, Submitted).
  • Projective Geometry for Perfectoid Spaces (2018, PhD Thesis, longer version of the paper above, including more exposition and foundation).
  • The Combinatorics of Interval Vector Polytopes. (2012, E. Journal of Combinatorics, Joint with Matthias Beck, Jessica De Silva, Joseph Pruitt, and Amanda Ruiz.)
