Curriculum Vitae

  • I am a student of Max Lieblich, studying Algebraic Geometry and Number Theory. My interests include rigid analytic geometry, and studying the foundational geometry of perfectoid spaces. My work includes developing a notion of projective geometry for perfectoid spaces, and producing examples of such objects. I'm mainly interested in applications to p-adic hodge theory, the Langlands program, and studying the Brauer group.
