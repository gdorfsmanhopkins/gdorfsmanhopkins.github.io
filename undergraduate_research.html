Undergraduate Research

For 3 years I mentored the Number Theory and Noise project at the WXML, together with Professor Matt Conroy. In this project we turn positive integer sequence (e.g., the primes) into sounds where the first million integers can be heard in 30 seconds! We have generated many sounds over the year, as well as uploaded many new sequences to the OEIS. Here is a description of how we turn a positive integer sequence into a sound.
